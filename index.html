Our frictionless generation protocol makes you earn passive income without having to do anything at all. 5% of every transaction automatically goes to holders, 5% goes to marketing. Our features

Verified Smart Contract

We Publish our Smart Contract source code, which means you can observe, and review the actual code in our contract. This gives buyers confidence, that they are buying into something legitimate, and genuine. You can review contract logic, parameters, etc.

Massive Marketing Plans will be in place

5% of every transaction is allocated to Marketing in BNB (swapped to BNB in real time to avoid dumps). Therefore, we can promote the most ambitious projects, and reward our active communities. We will also have both Pre, and Post Marketing plans.

We want to protect our holders from Whales, who snipe early, and then sell high ruining the experience for everyone else. We have employed a 4% Max Wallet, with 2% Max transaction which will prevent huge transactions at any one time.

The longer you hold $QUAD tokens, the more you earn in rewards! Simply buy & hold $QUAD and you’ll get $XRP airdropped to your wallet every 1 hours automatically.
